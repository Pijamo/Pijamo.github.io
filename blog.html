HOW WEB DESIGN AFFECTS SEO?

Do you want your website to rank better on the search engine results page (SERP)?

Search engine optimization or SEO consists of so many factors. Focusing on essential factors can boost search engine rankings. One of the crucial factors is web design.

Your website design can have a significant impact on its rankings. Here are some points explaining the role of web design in SEO:

Navigation

When someone lands your website, the first thing the user notices is design. Navigation is a vital part of web designing. Search engines expect easy navigation from websites. By adding proper navigation, you are helping your visitors in finding what they are looking for.

User experience

Your website design can improve the experience of website visitors. Improving user experience won’t just satisfy your visitors, but it will also help you boost your SEO effort. When your website design is good, people will get a better user experience. This will result in a low bounce rate. And, the low bounce rate means increased SEO effort.

Images

A good website isn’t just about textual content; it is also about visual content like images. Adding visual content on your website like images and videos won’t just engage your users; it will also boost your SEO effort. Search engines are more likely to rank websites with visual as well as textual content, rather than ones with just textual content.

Website loading speed

The way your website is designed creates a significant impact on its loading speed. If your website takes so long to load, users are more likely to bounce back and switch to your competitor’s website. An expert web designer knows how to design a website in such a manner that it can load faster.

From the above explanation, we can say that website design can significantly impact search engine rankings of the website. If you want to improve your SEO effort, and make your website look appealing, focus on web designing.

Good design is good business.
